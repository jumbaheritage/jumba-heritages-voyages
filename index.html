Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your safari packages?

Our safari packages include private transportation in our luxury 4x4 safari vehicles (Kenyan registered, K-plate), services of professional English-speaking driver-guides, park entry fees, and game drives as per itinerary. Accommodation and meals are curated based on your budget preferences - we work with you to select lodges and camps that match your desired luxury level.

How does accommodation and food work?

Accommodation and meals are strictly based on the client's budget and preferences. We offer a range of options from luxury tented camps to premium lodges and beach resorts. All accommodation and food/drinks are customized on a quote basis - simply let us know your preferences, and we'll prepare a personalized proposal. We do not include accommodation in the base transport price to give you maximum flexibility.

Do you offer transportation only?

Yes, absolutely. Jumba Heritage Voyages provides premium transportation services to all destination sites across Kenya. Whether you need airport transfers, multi-day safari transport, or point-to-point travel between parks, our luxury fleet and professional drivers are at your service. We can customize pure transport packages or full-service safari experiences.

What is your group size policy?

We specialize in small group luxury safaris with a maximum of 6-8 guests per vehicle, ensuring personalized attention and window seats for everyone. Private safaris can be arranged for solo travelers, couples, or families. Minimum group size for our published packages is 4 guests, but we're happy to customize for smaller groups at adjusted rates.

How do I book and what payment methods do you accept?

Booking is easy - simply click the "Inquire Now" button on any package, call us directly, or send a WhatsApp message. We accept M-PESA (Safaricom: 0710 731 785), Airtel Money (0789 514 678), bank transfers, and cryptocurrency (BTC/USDT) upon request. A deposit secures your booking, with balance payable before safari commencement.

Policies

Terms & Conditions

1. Booking and Confirmation

All bookings are confirmed upon receipt of a 30% deposit of the total safari cost. The remaining balance is due 30 days prior to safari commencement. For bookings made within 30 days of travel, full payment is required at the time of booking. Deposits can be made via M-PESA, Airtel Money, bank transfer, or cryptocurrency.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 60 days before departure: 90% refund of deposit. Cancellations between 30-60 days: 50% refund of deposit. Cancellations less than 30 days: no refund. However, you may transfer your booking to another person or future date subject to availability.

3. Accommodation and Meals

Jumba Heritage Voyages provides transportation and safari guiding services. Accommodation and meals are arranged based on client preferences and budget. We act as an intermediary between clients and accommodation providers. All accommodation and food expenses are quoted separately and are the responsibility of the client. We can customize all accommodation and dining on a quote basis upon request.

4. Transportation Services

Our luxury safari vehicles are Kenyan registered (K-plate), fully insured, and equipped with all necessary safety features. Professional driver-guides are licensed by the Kenya Tourism Board. Vehicle breakdowns, while rare, are covered by our 24/7 support network with replacement vehicles dispatched promptly.

5. Client Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for obtaining valid passports, visas, and any required vaccinations. Travel insurance is strongly recommended and should cover cancellation, medical expenses, and personal belongings. Jumba Heritage Voyages cannot be held liable for any injury, loss, or damage sustained during the safari.

6. Pricing Guarantee

All prices quoted are per person in USD and include government taxes and park fees where applicable. Prices are subject to change only in the event of government tax increases or significant currency fluctuations. We guarantee prices once deposit is received.
