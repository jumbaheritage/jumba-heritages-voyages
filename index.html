Your Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your safari packages?

Private transportation in luxury 4x4 safari vehicles, professional driver-guides, park entry fees, and game drives. Accommodation is customized to your budget.

How does accommodation and food work?

Accommodation is based on your budget - from luxury tented camps to premium lodges. All accommodation is customized on a quote basis.

Do you offer transportation only?

Yes, we provide premium transportation services to all Kenyan destinations.

What vehicles do you use?

Custom-modified Toyota Land Cruisers with pop-up roofs, WiFi, and refreshments. All Kenyan registered (K-plates).

How do I book and what payment methods do you accept?

Click "Inquire Now" on any package, call us, or send a WhatsApp. We accept M-PESA, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency.
